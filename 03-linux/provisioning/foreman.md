# Foreman

## What is Foreman?

Foreman is an open-source lifecycle management platform for physical and virtual servers.

It provides:

- Provisioning (PXE, Kickstart)
- Configuration management
- Inventory management
- Content management
- Ansible integration

## Related Technologies

- Red Hat Satellite
- Katello
- Kickstart
- PXE
- Ansible
- RHEL
